Learning Contract

Expectations

  1. I expect to learn new knowledge and skills.
  2. I expect everyone to participate actively.
  3. I expect to improve my ability to work independently.
  4. I expect to receive clear instructions and feedback.
  5. I expect to apply what I learn in real-life situations.

Motivations

  1. I want to do well to improve my grades.
  2. I am motivated to learn something new.
  3. I want to prove to myself that I can work independently.
  4. I am motivated by the sense of accomplishment after finishing a challenging task.
  5. I want to gain skills that will help me in the future.

Contributions

  1. I will complete my tasks on time and with effort.
  2. I will stay focused and avoid distractions.
  3. I will share my ideas when needed.
  4. I will follow the rules and instructions.
  5. I will help maintain a positive and respectful environment.

Hindrances

  1. I might get distracted by my surroundings.
  2. I may feel unmotivated if the task seems too hard.
  3. I might have limited time due to other responsibilities.
  4. I may lack confidence in my answers.
  5. I might struggle to understand the instructions clearly.
